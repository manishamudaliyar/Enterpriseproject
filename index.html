About Us

We delight our clients with innovative and sustainable packaging solutions and products.

VRaag Enterprise, founded in 2021, is a leader in Paper – Board Printing & Packaging and Corrugated Packaging. The company manufactures Mono Cartons, Duplex Board Cartons, Corrugated Master Cartons, Marketing Material (Mailers, Danglers, Stickers, Posters, Brochures, Catalogues), Instruction Manuals, Booklets, Annual Reports, Blister Cards, Corrugated Trays & Pads.

Our Strategy

VRaag Enterprise provides Printing & Packaging solutions and products to clients across sectors including Fast Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG), Food & Beverages, Pharmaceuticals, Electronic Goods, Retail, E-Commerce, Textile, Cosmetics and Footwear.



We focus on delivering consistent growth through four strategic value drivers:

– Optimize performance along the value chain

– Invest in assets with cost advantage

We believe in constant improvement and have systems in place that encourage all team members to innovate and create out-of-the-box solutions. We run rigorous quality management and operational excellence programs that enhance productivity and efficiency.

Investing in a sustainable portfolio of assets with the prime objective of having a cost advantage gives us an edge over our competitors and has made us an industry leader.

– Inspire our people and grow responsibly

– Partner with customers for innovation

Building the strongest team in the industry is one of our key missions. We believe diversity and the right training opportunities are key to the company’s future.

Working with our customers to create innovative solutions is key to our long-term success. Our focus is on enhancing the printing quality and producing lighter weight packaging materials without sacrificing strength.
